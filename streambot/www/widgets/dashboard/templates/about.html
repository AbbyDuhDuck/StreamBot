StreamBot Dashboard

Welcome to the StreamBot Dashboard! Here you can manage your stream settings, view analytics, and customize your stream experience.

Features

  • Stream Settings: Configure your stream title, category, and tags.
  • Analytics: View real-time statistics about your viewers, chat activity, and more.
  • Customization: Customize your stream layout, alerts, and widgets.

Getting Started

To get started, navigate to the settings page to configure your stream. You can also explore the analytics section to see how your stream is performing.
